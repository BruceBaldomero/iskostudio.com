About

I'm Bruce — the designer behind ISKO Studio.

When you work with ISKO, you work directly with me. The person who designs your site is the same person who builds it, launches it and is there afterwards — so you're never passed to an account manager or left chasing for an update.

I started ISKO because too many small businesses are stuck with slow, outdated websites — or none at all. You shouldn't need a big budget or a tech background to look the part online. So I keep things clean, fast and fairly priced, and I handle all the technical stuff for you.

The site is yours — your domain, your content. If you'd like, I'll keep it looked after every month. If not, that's fine too. No lock-in.
