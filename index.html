About

I'm Bruce. I build websites for businesses that mean business.

ISKO Studio is just me — which means the person who designs your site is the same person who builds it, launches it, and looks after it. No handoffs, no account managers, no chasing.

I started ISKO because too many small businesses are stuck with slow, outdated websites — or none at all. You shouldn't need a big budget or a tech background to look the part online. So I keep things clean, fast and fairly priced, and I handle all the technical stuff for you.

Build it once, look after it for life. That's the idea.
