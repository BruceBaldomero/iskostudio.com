What every care plan includes

  • Hosting — your site stays online, fast and secure
  • Around-the-clock uptime monitoring — alerted the moment anything goes down
  • Monthly contact-form testing — so you never silently lose an enquiry
  • Security and dependency updates, and automated backups
  • Small monthly edits — text, photos, copy changes (amount varies by tier)
  • A short monthly report — uptime, speed, what was done, anything fixed
  • Email support — faster response on higher tiers

Because your site is hand-built and lightweight — not a heavy WordPress install — it's fast and secure by design. No plugin bloat, no surprise breakages, no inflated maintenance bills.

What it doesn't cover

The care plan is for maintenance and small edits. Bigger work — new pages, new features, a redesign — is quoted separately. Care-plan clients always get a discount on that work.

Need more? Optional add-ons available on request: extra edit time, content uploads, Google Business profile management, additional pages. Just ask.

FAQ

Common questions.

Are changes to my site included in the care plan?

Small edits — updating text, swapping a photo, tweaking wording — are included each month. The exact amount depends on your tier. Bigger work, like new pages, new features or a redesign, is quoted separately; care-plan clients always get a discount on that.

Do I get a monthly report?

Yes. Each month you'll receive a short report covering uptime, page speed, what maintenance was carried out, and anything that was fixed. Nothing overwhelming — just the things that matter.

Do you write my content for me?

I can advise on structure and help you shape your words, but the written content and images for your site need to come from you — you know your business best. I'll make sure everything looks great once it's in.

Do you set up Google Analytics and Search Console?

Yes — Google Analytics and Search Console setup is included as standard. This lets us track visitors, monitor search performance, and catch any technical issues (broken links, mobile usability, core web vitals) early.

Is SEO marketing included?

Every site is built with solid technical SEO foundations — clean code, fast loading, proper headings, meta tags, and Google Business setup. Ongoing SEO marketing (content strategy, link building, paid campaigns) is a specialist discipline and isn't included, but I can point you in the right direction.

Who owns my website, domain, and accounts?

You do — always. Your domain, your hosting account, your Google profiles, your content. I never lock you in to accounts you can't access. Once the project is paid for, everything is yours.

Will you renew my domain or hosting?

Your domain is registered in your name and renewed by you directly with your registrar — I'll remind you when it's coming up. Hosting is included in the care plan, so as long as you're on a plan, the site stays online. If you ever leave, I'll help you migrate everything cleanly.

What if I find a bug?

All sites come with a 30-day bug-fix warranty after launch — any issue that's down to my build gets fixed at no extra cost. After that, small bugs are covered by your care plan; larger ones are quoted separately.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Care plans are month-to-month with 30 days' notice — no lock-in, no penalty. Your site and all your files remain yours.

How do I request a change or get in touch?

Just email contact@iskostudio.com. I aim to respond within one working day — faster on higher-tier plans. For new projects, use the contact form on this page.
