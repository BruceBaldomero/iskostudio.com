Common questions

How much does a website cost in Kingston?

Starter from £500, Standard from £850, Premium from £1,400, plus an optional care plan from £50/mo. The quote is fixed before we start and doesn't move once the work begins.

Should I use a freelancer or an agency?

An agency adds cost and layers you rarely need for a local business. Working with a solo designer means the person who quotes you is the person who builds and looks after your site — leaner, quicker, and more personal.

What's the turnaround?

Roughly 1–2 weeks for a Starter site, 2–3 for Standard and 3–4 for Premium, from the point your content is ready. You'll have a firm launch date confirmed before any work starts.

Are you only available to Kingston businesses?

Not at all. I regularly work with businesses in Surbiton, Norbiton, New Malden, Hampton Wick and Ham, and remotely beyond. Being nearby simply makes meeting in person an easy option.
