Common questions

How much does a website cost in Surbiton?

Sites start at £500 for Starter, £850 for Standard and £1,400 for Premium, with an optional care plan from £50/mo. You get a fixed quote before anything starts, and the price doesn't change once we begin.

Freelancer or agency — which do I actually need?

Agencies suit big budgets and big teams. For a local business, a good freelancer gives you the same quality of work without the overheads or the layers of account managers — and you deal directly with the person building your site. That's what you get with ISKO.

How long does it take?

Starter sites take 1–2 weeks, Standard 2–3 weeks and Premium 3–4 weeks, measured from when you've got your content to me. We agree a firm launch date before we start.

Do you only work with Surbiton businesses?

No — I work with businesses across Kingston, Tolworth, New Malden, Berrylands and Thames Ditton, and remotely further afield. Being local just means I can meet you in person if you'd prefer.
